Sample jobs

Projects sized for normal homes

Front-yard reset

Swap out worn turf for gravel, a short path, and a few sturdy anchor plants.

  • Best fit: Older front yards with patchy grass.
  • Scope: Simple curb-appeal update.

Side-yard cleanup

Add compacted gravel, one shade tree, and a more usable walking path.

  • Best fit: Long, dusty side yards.
  • Scope: Access and maintenance improvement.

Patio border refresh

Frame a small patio with gravel, edging, and low-water planting.

  • Best fit: Small backyards that feel unfinished.
  • Scope: Warmth without overbuilding.
