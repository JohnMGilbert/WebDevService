Approach

Feature work should support the site, not distract from it.

I keep the flow clear, match the feature to the business need, and avoid adding things that increase maintenance without helping visitors or the owner.

  • Forms and workflows mapped to how inquiries actually come in
  • Integrations chosen for maintainability, not novelty
  • Layouts that keep the feature understandable on smaller screens
  • Room for future expansion without rebuilding the whole site
