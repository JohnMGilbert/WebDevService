Short description

Southwestern yards with restraint, rhythm, and real shade.

Desert Yards Landscaping is a fictional boutique crew that reworks tired gravel lots, thirsty turf, and awkward side yards into calm outdoor rooms. The look is regional and practical: decomposed granite, sculptural agave, handmade tile, steel edging, pavers, mesquite shade, and lighting that makes the yard useful after sunset.

Fake bio

Marisol Vega, founder and lead designer.

Marisol is a fictional landscape designer raised between Tucson and Las Cruces. After years of replacing thirsty lawns with shade-forward xeriscapes, she started Desert Yards to make practical desert planting feel composed, personal, and quietly refined.

Her crew keeps the process direct: walk the site, map the sun, simplify the irrigation, choose plants that belong here, and build a yard that looks better as it settles in.
