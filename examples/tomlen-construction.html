Las Cruces contractor FAQ

Start with the questions that shape the work.

Do you work across the Las Cruces area?

Yes. The service area includes Las Cruces, Mesilla, Dona Ana County, and nearby Southern New Mexico communities.

What should I include when I request a quote?

Share the project type, address or neighborhood, photos if available, timing goals, known concerns, and the best way to reach you.

Can stucco repairs be matched to the existing finish?

Texture and color matching are part of the planning conversation. The goal is to repair the underlying issue and blend the finished surface as cleanly as possible.

When should I consider a roof coating?

A properly prepared coating system can help protect flat and low-slope roofs when the existing surface, drainage, seams, and penetrations are good candidates for coating.
